Dharma Chakra Centre

Rumtek Monastery

Near Gangtok, East Sikkim

The largest monastery in Sikkim and the main seat of the Karma Kagyu lineage outside Tibet. Known as the "Dharma Chakra Centre," it houses precious relics and serves as a center for Buddhist learning and meditation practices.

Perfect Sublime Lotus

Pemayangtse Monastery

Near Pelling, West Sikkim

One of the oldest and most important monasteries in Sikkim, meaning "Perfect Sublime Lotus." It's the head monastery of the Nyingma order and offers stunning views of Kanchenjunga mountain range.

Devoted Central Glory

Tashiding Monastery

West Sikkim

Perched on a hilltop between Rathong and Rangeet rivers, this sacred monastery is believed to cleanse sins of devotees. It's famous for the holy water ceremony during Bhumchu festival.

Blooming Lotus

Phodong Monastery

North Sikkim

A beautiful monastery of the Kagyu sect, rebuilt after the original was destroyed by earthquake. Known for its annual Chaam dance during the festival season and serene mountain setting.

Solitary Temple

Enchey Monastery

Gangtok, East Sikkim

Located on a hilltop in Gangtok, this 200-year-old monastery belongs to the Nyingma order. It's famous for its annual Chaam dance and offers panoramic views of Kanchenjunga.

Small Heaven

Ralang Monastery

Ravangla, South Sikkim

A serene monastery of the Kagyu order, known for its peaceful atmosphere and beautiful location. It's an important center for Buddhist learning and meditation practices in South Sikkim.

Scenic Viewpoint

Kaalapathar, Sikkim

Near Tsomgo Lake, East Sikkim

Kaalapathar is a stunning viewpoint near Tsomgo Lake in East Sikkim, known for its mesmerizing views of snow-covered mountains, pine forests, and valleys. It's a popular stop for tourists seeking natural beauty and photography opportunities.

Indo-China Border

Nathula Pass, Sikkim

East Sikkim, Indo-China Border

Nathula Pass, located at 14,140 ft on the Indo-China border, is one of the highest motorable roads in the world. Known for its strategic importance and breathtaking views of the Himalayas, it attracts tourists for its natural beauty and historical significance. Visitors can also see Chinese soldiers across the border.
